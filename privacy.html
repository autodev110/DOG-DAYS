DOG DAYS POLICY

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 29, 2026

DOG DAYS keeps this site static. The store remembers only what the browser is asked to hold.

1. Browser Records

Cart contents may be saved in local browser storage so selected goods remain present after a refresh. A completed receipt may also remain in the same browser as an order artifact.

2. Accounts And Profiles

DOG DAYS does not operate customer accounts, behavioral profiles, mailing lists, measurement profiles, or shipment records through this site.

3. Payment Handling

The checkout interface does not request card numbers, banking credentials, passwords, or delivery addresses. Authorization choices are used only to classify the order notice inside the page.

4. Clearing Records

Browser records can be cleared by removing cart items, completing checkout, or clearing site storage in the browser. DOG DAYS accepts the disappearance of these records as ordinary weather.

5. Contact

Contact is unavailable when the store is unavailable. The policy remains posted for reference.
